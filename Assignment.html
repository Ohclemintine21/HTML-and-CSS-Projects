All about Orca whales Orca whales AKA "killer whales"
Apex Predators

Orca are dominate predators of the sea or Apex predators. They are very skilled and adaptable hunters and their diets can range from fish, seals, sharks, to even dolphins.

As John Hargrove once said and I quote:

"Nothing in the ocean hunts killer whales as prey"
